Social Media Metrics Checkpoint

Identify which metric best matches each social media interaction.

1. A brand's Instagram post receives 350 likes and reactions.

2. A LinkedIn post generates 42 comments and replies from followers.

3. A TikTok video is shared 180 times by viewers.

4. A Facebook post receives likes, comments, shares, and clicks.

5. A YouTube community post receives many user replies discussing the topic.

6. A product announcement is reposted by customers across their own networks.

7. A social media dashboard combines all likes, comments, shares, and clicks for one campaign.

8. A brand post receives hundreds of heart reactions from followers.

Content Strategy Studio

Practice turning keyword research into a simple content plan. You will review one keyword at a time, decide its search intent, choose the best content format, and watch your strategy board fill in as you work.

Scenario

You are helping a marketing team at an email marketing software company. The team already completed keyword research and now needs help deciding what kind of content to create for each keyword opportunity.

How It Works

  • Review one keyword card at a time.
  • Select the best search intent.
  • Select the best content format.
  • Submit your decision for each keyword.
  • Watch the content board fill automatically by topic cluster.

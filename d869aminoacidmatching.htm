Amino Acid Classification Lab

Practice grouping amino acids according to the chemical properties of their R-groups. You will review one amino acid at a time, select its category, and watch the classification board fill as you complete the activity.

Purpose

R-group properties influence how amino acids interact with water, other amino acids, and their surrounding environment. These interactions contribute to protein structure, stability, and function.

How It Works
  • Review one amino acid structure at a time.
  • Examine the highlighted R-group.
  • Select the matching R-group category.
  • Submit the classification to receive feedback.
  • Continue until all amino acids are classified.
